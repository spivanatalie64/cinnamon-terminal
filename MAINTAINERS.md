## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review and merge merge requests
- Triage and respond to issues
- Cut releases according to the release schedule
- Handle security reports per SECURITY.md
- Keep dependencies and upstream tracking up to date
- Maintain CI/CD pipeline health

## Adding a Maintainer

To add a new maintainer, a current maintainer must propose the addition
via a merge request. Existing maintainers will vote. Simple majority wins.

## Removing a Maintainer

A maintainer may step down at any time by opening an issue.
A maintainer may be removed by unanimous vote of remaining maintainers
if they have been inactive for 6+ months.
